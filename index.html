About Me

My introduction

I thought I would be a physical therapist for a long time, in my scrubs helping people live a healthier and functional life. This seemed in place given that I was best in my class and love caring for people. Well, I accidentally stumbled on a JavaScript video which ignited an unusual spark in me, turned my life around and here I am coding for a living. I won't change a thing about my experiences because I believe they shape the way I view the world, break down problems and communicate solutions.

I love a good challenge and the yes!! moment that comes with it. I love what I do, and mostly the fact that my job gives me the thrill that makes me feel so powerful as well as challenges me enough to question whether I am on the right track. My specialties include quickly learning new skills and technologies. So far, I have HTML, CSS, JavaScript/TypeScript, JQuery, React, SvelteKit, Node.JS, SQL, MongoDb, Docker, Git/GitHub under belt. I have started learning Python/Flask and still actively grabbing onto any principles that I can that would make my job easier and more efficient.
